Shopify's draft order feature is great for invoicing customer with customized line items, but the downside is that the invoice links (for customer to pay for the invoice) are sent by email to the customer inbox, and these emails might land into spam, lost in transit, or not even sent due to Shopify's issue. And customer have to comb through their emails (along with customer support emails) to find the invoice email.
