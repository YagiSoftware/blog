Previously, Shopify does not allow setting draft status for product variant, which can cause inconvenience, as the variant is automatically made public when you create it in the Shopify Admin.

Sometimes there's dozens of product variants scheduled to launch in the future, you want to input the variant description and image now, set it to draft, and launch later, instead of panic adding all the info and images on the launch date! 

This used to require complex coding modification on the theme frontend, but [Shopify has since supported sales channel publishing on the product variant](https://changelog.shopify.com/posts/product-variant-publishing), which you can now unpublish product variant from the online store sales channel, the variant will be hidden on the online store, which is the same as setting it as "Draft".

To unpublish a product variant (equivalent to set as draft), open the product detail page in your Shopify Admin, scroll to the variants list, and click the icons on the "Publishing" column.

![Select publishing option for product variants](https://img.yagisoftware.com/34-product-variant-draft/1_publishing.png)

Uncheck the "Online Stores" sales channel (and other applicable sales channel) : 

![Disable online store for product variant](https://img.yagisoftware.com/34-product-variant-draft/2_disable_online_store.png)

Click "Save", then navigate to the product page, and you should see that variant is removed.
